Notion Date AutoFiller

Chrome Web Store: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/notion-date-filler/fejlopadadjhidmmmggdkbipcpgedgjp

Notion Date AutoFiller is the fastest way to populate date columns in your Notion databases.

Stop manually entering dates one by one. Select a range, hit Copy, and paste hundreds of dates instantly into Notion — no limits, no empty rows.

✨ What's New in v1.1.0:
✔️ Payment Mode — Generate recurring monthly payment dates (e.g. every 15th) across any range
✔️ Full-year support — Copy 300+ dates at once without truncation
✔️ Dark & Light mode — Remembers your preference across sessions
✔️ Quick ranges — Jump to Week, Month, or Year in one click

⚡ Core Features:
✔️ Set custom Start & End dates with ease
✔️ Exclude specific days of the week (perfect for skipping weekends)
✔️ One-click Copy — dates go straight to your clipboard, ready to paste into any Notion database
✔️ Lightweight, fast, and works entirely offline

Built for Notion power users, freelancers, and anyone managing recurring schedules, payments, or project timelines.

Available for: Chrome, Brave, and Edge.
